Gamers4Change

Gamers4Change was founded in 2014 when a group of streamers decided to band together and raise money for charity. In 2015, Gamers4Change raised over $170,000 for charity. In 2016, we hope to increase that to $100,000 for charity through three major charity events, as well as several smaller events. We provide overlays, alert graphics, and support to streamers that want to get into Charity streaming. In order to be a member of Gamers4Change, we ask that you agree to participate in 1 of our 3 major charity events a year. (But we would love for you to participate in all events.) This team provides organization with collaboration for charity streamers.
