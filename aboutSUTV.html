Timeline

StreamersUnityTV Was previously known as KlumaArmy which can be referred to in this Time Line

December 2015

Team Page Born

30 Streamers Within the community of Klumaa was invited to be part of a Web Site based team which was including streamers who supported Klumaa and then was supporting other small streamers that needed help growing and branching out!

Oct 10th 2016

Twitch Team Born

KlumaArmy was born! The Team was dedicated to steamers who supported and raised money for charity events for St Jude, Extra Life and more

2017

We Started to branch out to gain more support from other teams and have reached out to RGN Team and Gamers4Change to help support streamers raise funds.
